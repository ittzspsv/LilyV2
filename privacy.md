# Privacy Policy — Lily

**Last updated: June 2026**

---

## What is Lily?

Lily is a multipurpose Discord bot built to help servers with moderation, utility commands, ticket handling, and staff management. This policy explains what data Lily collects, why it needs it, and how it's handled. It's written to be straightforward — no legal jargon you need a lawyer to decode.

By adding Lily to your server, you're agreeing to what's described below.

---

## What data we collect

Lily only collects what it actually needs to function. Nothing more.

**Discord account information**

- User ID (your unique Discord identifier)
- Username
- Avatar URL

---

**Server information**

- Server ID (so Lily knows which server it's operating in)
- Channel IDs (used to configure features like moderation logging)

---

**Stored data** _(only when the relevant feature is enabled)_

- Server configuration settings — things like roles, prefixes, and feature toggles
- Moderation logs — warnings, bans, mutes, and similar actions
- Ticket and support system data

---

**Staff Management System**

To support staff management within your server, we collect and store the following data:

- **Staff Records** — When a staff member is added to the system, we record the date they joined. Timezones may also be stored, though this is entirely optional.
- **Infractions** — If a staff member is issued an infraction, we store the date it was issued, the reason it was given, and its expiry date.
- **Quota Configuration** — Server administrators may configure quotas on a per-role basis. This includes the role the quota applies to, the type of quota, and the conditions that define whether it has been fulfilled.

---

**Leveling System**

To power the leveling system, we collect and store message activity counts. This includes

- **daily, weekly, monthly**, and **total** message counts per user.

It is important to note that only the counts themselves are stored — the content of your messages is never read, logged, or retained in any form.

---

**Ticketing System**

When a ticket is closed, all messages within it are compiled and converted into an HTML transcript, which is then posted directly to your **server's designated ticket transcripts channel**. We only store a **reference to that message** — not the **transcript content itself** — to allow it to be retrieved later if needed.

You retain full control over your ticket transcripts and may delete them at any time.

---

## How we use it

All collected data is used strictly to make Lily work. That means:

- Running commands and features
- Storing server configurations between sessions
- Maintaining moderation and logging history
- Identifying and preventing abuse or misuse

We don't sell your data. We don't rent it. We don't share it with advertisers or any third parties for their benefit.

---

## Data storage and retention

We hold onto data for as long as it's needed to provide Lily's features. A few specifics worth knowing:

- Server owners can request deletion of their server's data at any time
- Data from servers that have been inactive for an extended period may be periodically removed
- When you remove Lily from a server, data associated with that server is eligible for deletion on request

---

## Data sharing

We don't share your data with outside parties. The only exceptions are situations where we're legally required to — for example, to comply with a valid legal order or to meet Discord's own Terms of Service obligations.

That's it. No analytics partners, no data brokers, nothing like that.

---

## Your rights

You have real control over your data. You can:

- **Access** — ask us what information we have about you or your server
- **Correct** — request a fix if something we've stored is wrong or outdated
- **Delete** — ask us to remove your data entirely
- **Export** — request your data in a portable format
- **Restrict** — ask us to limit how your data is processed in certain situations
- **Object** — push back on specific types of data processing

Server administrators can also disable individual features that store data if they'd prefer Lily not to retain certain information at all.

To make any of these requests, reach out through our [Support Server](https://discord.com/invite/K7Ad5JssCg).

---

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll let you know — either through an announcement in the support server or via a bot update message. Continuing to use Lily after a policy update means you're okay with the changes.
